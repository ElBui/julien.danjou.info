--- title: "VARmon" --- {% mark excerpt %} *VARMon* is a text-based tool to monitor DAC 960/1100 based RAID controllers. {% endmark %} # History As the Debian maintainer of *VARMon*, I wrote several patches for it. Dragan Stanvecic, the original author did not want to continue maintenance of *VARMon*, therefore I took over its maintenance in 2004. # Download You can grab it from the [Git](http://git.naquadah.org/?p=varmon.git;a=summary) repository.
