--- title: Icon category support in Org-mode created: !!timestamp '2010-11-04 13:11:00' image: /media/images/blog/2010/emacs-org-category-icons.png tags: - emacs - orgmode --- {% mark excerpt %} My latest patch for [Org mode](http://orgmode.org) has been accepted by Carsten today. It adds support for custom category icons in all views, like agenda or todo. {% endmark %} You just need to configure *org-agenda-category-icon-alist* and it will work out of the box. ![Org-mode category support screenshot]({{site.url}}/media/images/blog/2010/emacs-org-category-icons.png)
