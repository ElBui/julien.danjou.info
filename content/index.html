Hello!

I am a Free Software hacker and a Freelance Engineer. For the past 10 years I've been both a software engineer and a Linux systems engineer.

I've contributed to a lot of projects and developed some free software. I also spent several years working as a Linux systems administrator and engineer for various organizations and companies.

You can read my resume online. Feel free to contact me.

Nowadays, I'm mainly interested in X11, functional programming languages, massive data storage and computing scalability.
